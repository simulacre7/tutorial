My list panel

"My list" panel

The My list panel is a list of temporary candidates like a shopping cart. If you find interesting movies on other panels, you can put them here by clicking plus button. This allows you to manage your candidates without having to remember them.

Our system recommends similar movies to those in the My list. Whenever, you change the My list, the Recommendations & Random movies panels are automatically updated.
