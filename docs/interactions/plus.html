Plus & Minus button

Plus & Minus button


The Plus & Minus button appears, when you mouse-hover the movie poster.
The purpose of these buttons is to add or remove a movie to My list. For this reason, The Plus button appears in only panels other than the My list, on the other hand, the Minus button appears in only the My list.
